The Challenge

Our Solution

A robust CNN model trained on the GTSRB dataset, integrated with a real-time video processing pipeline that stabilizes predictions and filters noise.

Engineering Challenges

Color Confusion

Problem: OpenCV reads in BGR, but our training logic (PIL) interprets data differently, causing Red borders (Speed Limits) to look Blue to the model.

Solved: RGB/BGR Consistency

Prediction Flicker

Problem: In live video, predictions jump wildly (e.g., "80" -> "100" -> "80") due to sensor noise and angle shifts.

Solved: Deque Buffer

False Positives

Problem: The model forcing a prediction on background objects (trees, walls), identifying them as signs.

Solved: 80% Threshold
